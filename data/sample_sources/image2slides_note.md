# Image2Slides content handoff workflow

Image2Slides is a content production workflow for turning source material and visual plans into editable slide decks. The useful product idea is the separation between source-locked facts, generated narrative structure, and final editable outputs.

A reliable content workflow should keep a source registry, a content boundary, a slide plan, and validation reports together. The draft can be creative, but factual panels and claims need a source id. Final outputs should be easy to inspect by a human reviewer before publishing or presenting.

This makes the workflow suitable for knowledge-to-brief, knowledge-to-slide-plan, and knowledge-to-article tasks where traceability matters more than unchecked generation speed.
